# Inclusion Exclusion Criteria

## Inclusion

1. The paper must be in English
2. A paper should tackle at least one of the research questions of ours
3. The paper should focus on small or medium sized organizations/institutions
4. The paper should use tangible and measurable statistics (like hours saved, KPIs achieved etc.)

## Exclusion

1. The paper cannot be a literature review
2. The paper should not focus on countries or Multi-National Corporations

## Reasoning

- Focus on small & medium sized enterprises adds focus to the paper and grounds an otherwise broad topic
- Excluding countries is a means to avoid taking governments into account as I wish to covering I.T. in defense spending.
- Focusing on tangible and measurable statistics helps avoid marketing hyperbole or speculated gains of adopting certain tech/service.
